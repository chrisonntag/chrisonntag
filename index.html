September 2012    SunEdit

The miniCMS, developed by dialogsued is an easy to manage full content management system for every kind of website. To extend the flexibility and usability of miniCMS we created a simple and leightweight javascript based online text editor. SunEdit offers a WYSIWYG-mode where the user can see the written text and images as they will look like on his website. Advanced users can also use the HTML-Mode in which you can edit the content in HTML-Syntax.
